    • Equal House

    • by Colors of Technovation

    • Turkey

    • Our application creates a solution to gender inequality by making families share the chores at their home equally. Our app is for families or partners who are having a hard time doing their chores equally. Everyone should do an equal amount of chores. This way everyone will have less work to do and it will be much easier for the whole family. Everyone will have 1 or more jobs each day depending on how many tasks you want to. The app won’t know people’s gender, that’s why it will always be equal. When you first enter the app you will choose how many members you have and then you will write a chore that must be done, when you press shuffle it will reveal who must do that chore. Then, it will switch to another screen and reveal the chore and the name who must do it. When the person finishes the chore, that person will press on the button and the chore will be red which means that the chore is finished.

    • Collecting Memory

    • by Animo

    • Turkey

    • Retrograde amnesia is the loss of the memory of events that occurred or information that was learned in one’s past. It is usually caused by the onset of a disease or an injury. It is not usually talked about in public since it’s not very common. However, it severely affects people who have to live with it, and even though recovery isn’t guaranteed with retrograde amnesia patients, with this project we aim to help them get better or struggle as little as possible while dealing with symptoms. ‘Collecting Memory’ is an app designed to help doctors and caretakers of retrograde amnesia patients track their progress and keep up with their treatment plan. Our app provides brain exercises and additional reminders etc. to help with all amnesia symptoms.
    • Program: Senior Division
    • Topic: Good Health and Well-being
